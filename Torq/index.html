About Us

Torque is the flagship technical fest of the Mechanical Engineering Department at University College of Engineering, Kakinada. Celebrating innovation, creativity, and engineering excellence, Torque is a platform where the brightest minds converge to showcase their technical prowess and problem-solving skills.

At its core, Torque is more than just a technical festâ€”it's a celebration of the mechanical engineering community. It fosters collaboration, knowledge sharing, and networking among students, faculty, and industry professionals. Through its events, Torque aims to ignite a passion for engineering, nurture talent, and prepare the next generation of innovators to tackle the challenges of tomorrow.
